About Kulhudhuffushi Port Limited

Kulhudhuffushi Port Limited (KPL) serves as a key regional gateway in the north of Maldives, connecting local communities and businesses to the rest of the country and the world.

Who We Are

KPL operates as a vital logistics and maritime hub for the northern atolls. From cargo handling and vessel operations to passenger movements and support services, KPL is dedicated to ensuring safe, efficient, and reliable port operations.

Located in Kulhudhuffushi City, our port supports trade, transportation, and economic growth for the wider region, working closely with government agencies, shipping lines, agents, and the local community.

Our Vision

Your world-class port in the Indian Ocean, bridging the East and West.

Our Mission

To provide safe, efficient, and reliable port services through innovation, sustainable best practices, and strong stakeholder engagement.

Our Core Values

Safety

We prioritize the safety of our staff, customers, vessels, and cargo in every operation.

Reliability

We work to ensure that port services run smoothly, on time, and with minimum disruption.

Service Excellence

We strive to deliver a professional, customer-focused experience at every touchpoint.

What We Do

  • Cargo and container handling for inter-island and domestic trade.
  • Vessel berthing, mooring, and port operations management.
  • Passenger and ferry terminal support for regional connections.
  • Storage and yard management for goods in transit.
  • Coordination with stakeholders on safety, security, and compliance.
